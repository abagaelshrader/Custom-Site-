Hi, I'm a teacher turned web developer and designer who loves clean and purposeful code.

A little bit about me.

I am a former teacher and life-long learner who fell in love with computer science by accident. Through years of piecing together lessons for students, I was fascinated by (and jealous of) the consistent excitement and possibility for innovative use.

When I learned how to build my first website, I realized that I found something that gave me the innovation and creativity I had been searching for in my career. Now I use my eye for detail, love of learning, and excellent organizational skills to write clean and purposeful code.

Hand-Coded HTML

My focus is writing clean, well-formatted, semantic HTML5 by hand to make sure that the content is easy to read, easy to collaborate, trouble-shoot, and accessible.

Well-Organized CSS

I pride myself on writing CSS that is easy to read and build on. I focus on keeping my CSS lean and fast to load, and I make it a habit to stay up to date on current best practices.

Ease Converting Designs into Code

You can trust me to take a designer's PSD and quickly & accurately convert it into a webpage that is pixel-perfect match.
